Sustainability

Advantages of a Cross-Laminated Timber (CLT) Wall with External High-Performance Insulation

High Structural Strength

Cross-laminated timber (CLT) panels provide exceptional structural performance, dimensional stability, and durability. The crosswise arrangement of multiple timber layers creates a strong and rigid construction system capable of carrying substantial loads while maintaining long-term stability.

Excellent Energy Efficiency

The combination of CLT and external high-performance insulation creates a highly energy-efficient building envelope with excellent thermal performance and reduced heating and cooling costs.

Outstanding Thermal Comfort

External insulation minimizes thermal bridges and helps maintain stable indoor temperatures throughout the year, creating a comfortable living environment in both summer and winter.

Healthy Indoor Climate

Timber naturally contributes to a pleasant indoor atmosphere and helps regulate humidity levels, enhancing overall living comfort.

Sustainability and Low Carbon Footprint

CLT is produced from renewable wood resources and stores carbon throughout the life of the building. This significantly reduces the environmental impact compared to many conventional construction methods.

Fast Construction Process

CLT elements are prefabricated with high precision and assembled quickly on site, resulting in shorter construction times and improved quality control.

Improved Airtightness

Large-format CLT panels combined with external insulation facilitate the creation of a highly airtight building envelope, improving energy performance and reducing unwanted heat loss.

Excellent Acoustic Performance

CLT wall systems with external insulation provide very good sound insulation and a quiet, comfortable indoor environment.

Family Infrastructure

Location Benefits – Herbstweg, Gränichen

Education & Childcare

  • Several kindergartens are located within the municipality of Gränichen.
  • Primary and secondary schools are available directly in Gränichen.
  • Children can complete almost their entire compulsory education within the local school system.
  • Additional educational facilities, including higher-level schools, are easily accessible in nearby Aarau.

Public Transport

  • Nearby bus stops provide convenient connections within Gränichen and the surrounding region.
  • Gränichen railway station offers regular train services towards Aarau and the Wynental region.
  • Aarau city center can be reached in approximately 10–15 minutes by public transport or car.
  • Excellent connections to the Swiss national rail and road network.

Shopping & Daily Amenities

  • Migros supermarket
  • Coop supermarket
  • Denner discount store
  • Volg convenience store
  • LANDI market
  • Additional local shops, bakeries, pharmacies, banks, and services are located in the village center.

Recreation & Outdoor Activities

  • Extensive forests and walking trails begin just minutes from the development.
  • Beautiful cycling and hiking routes throughout the Wynental region.
  • The nearby Liebegg area offers one of the most attractive recreational landscapes in the region.
  • Multiple playgrounds, sports facilities, and family-friendly outdoor spaces.
  • Numerous opportunities for jogging, mountain biking, hiking, and nature activities.

Family-Friendly Environment

  • Quiet residential neighborhood with low traffic volumes.
  • Safe and child-friendly surroundings.
  • Strong local community and village character.
  • Excellent balance between rural tranquility and urban accessibility.

Summary

Herbstweg in Gränichen offers an ideal combination of family-friendly living, excellent educational facilities, convenient public transportation, comprehensive shopping amenities, and outstanding recreational opportunities. Residents benefit from a peaceful natural setting while remaining well connected to Aarau and the greater Zurich region.
